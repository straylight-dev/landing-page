The universe of Dune

Dune is a series set in the far future where humanity is ruled under a feudal system. Various factions are competing for the reigns of the universe.

Major components of Dune.

The Spacing Guild control the only means of travel between planets.

Spice melange the single most important material in Dune. Extends life, allows spaceflight and more.

The Sandworm a fierce, territorial beast. When extracting spice be on the lookout for them, its only a matter of time.

The bene Gesserit, a faction of women who have meticulously laid out a plan centuries in the making.

I know a profound pattern which humans deny with their words while their actions affirm it. They say they seek security and quiet, the condition they call peace. Even as they speak they create the seeds of turmoil and violence. If they find their quiet security, they squirm in it.

- Leto II, God Emperor of Dune
